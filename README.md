# Introduction-to-Cybersecurity-
What cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is protecting our computers, phones, and data from hackers and viruses. It’s like digital security that keeps our online information safe.

Why cybersecurity is important?

Cybersecurity is important because we do almost everything online today.If we don’t have cybersecurity, hackers can take your money, leak your photos, shut down hospitals, or spread lies.  
Good cybersecurity protects your info, keeps companies working, and stops scams.

Three examples

When we use banking apps,Encryption and 2FA stop hackers from accessing your account or stealing money.
On WhatsApp and email,encryption keeps our messages private. 
WiFi passwords on our phones to stop strangers from getting in our networks.
