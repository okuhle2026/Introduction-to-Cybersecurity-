# Introduction-to-Cybersecurity-
Cybersecurity is protecting our computers, phones, and data from hackers and viruses. It’s like digital security that keeps our online information safe.

Cybersecurity is important because we do almost everything online today.If we don’t have cybersecurity, hackers can take your money, leak your photos, shut down hospitals, or spread lies.  
Good cybersecurity protects your info, keeps companies working, and stops scams.

We use cybersecurity every day without realizing it. When we use banking apps, encryption and 2FA keep our money safe. On WhatsApp and email, encryption keeps our messages private. And WiFi passwords plus antivirus on our phones stop strangers and viruses from getting in.

I’m interested in being a Security Analyst. They watch for cyber attacks and respond when something goes wrong. I like it because it’s fast-paced, I get to solve problems, and I help keep people and companies safe.
